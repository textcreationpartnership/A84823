1. To the light of Chriſt in all your Conſciences, I do ſpeak which comes from the Lord Jeſus Chriſt, who enlightens every one that cometh into the world, which if you all do love it will lead you all from thoſe teachers aforementioned up to Chriſt from whence the light doth come, and hear is your way to ſalvation, and this is the light which ſhews you ſin and evill; if you love it, it will lead you to Chriſt from whence the light doth come, and if you hate it, it is your condemnation: ſo remember you are warned in your life time this is the day of your viſitation.
